About Us

King Asian Restaurant is a proud family-owned restaurant serving the Mississauga community with authentic Chinese cuisine. Every dish is prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're looking to dine in with family and friends, grab a quick take out order, or have a delicious meal delivered straight to your door, we're committed to bringing you flavorful, high-quality food every time.

Family Owned

A local family business dedicated to genuine hospitality and great food.

Fresh Ingredients
